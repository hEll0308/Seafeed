Our Vision

A world where ruminant livestock emmissions are no longer an issue.

Our goal is to protect our planet against harmful livestock emmissions. By creating a livestock additive feed out of the methane-reducing seaweed, Asapagopsis Taxisformis, our goal is to reduce ruminant emmsisions in an effective and eco-friendly manner.

Effective

Replacing 2% of a cow's diet with our feed can reduce upto 99% of methane.

Scalable

Using land-ocean hybrid aquaculture to maximize seaweed production.

Economically incentivized

AT seaweed can increase milk & meat production.

Eco-friendly

Seaweed growth can filter water and promote aquatic biodivesity
